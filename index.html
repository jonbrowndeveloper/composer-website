Jon Brown is a composer born and raised in Minneapolis, Minnesota. From a young age, he studied music for the piano and had a tendency to annoy those around him with his constant humming. Continuing on with his musical education, he study piano composition for four years at Bethel University in St. Paul, Minnesota where he then graduated with accolades from both his colleagues and professors alike.

Brown draws influence for his work from his depth of life experiences. His formative years were spent mastering the classics of Bach and Chopin and Mozart. His early love for film set ablaze a creative desire to compose music for motion picture, venerating greats like John Williams, Hans Zimmer, and Joe Hisaishi. In addition to original composition, Brown is involved in many other projects. He has pioneered the music department for Creative Love Church, acting as their main audio engineer. He has provided piano pedagogy to children, ages 6 to 13. And Brown has also produced audio for local singer-songwriters, podcasts and short films. In his spare-time, he still likes to hum.
